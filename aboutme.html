Hi, I'm Margreet

and I love fixing things. I also love to use my skills and creativity to give broken, old or unwanted things another life. It started many years ago when my mom showed me how to upholster an armchair and my kids asked me to fix their favourite toys. I have taken woodworking lessons and built some furniture for house and garden as well as lots of gifts for school fayres and Christmas decorations. During covid I got my first commission to repair a set of chairs which gave me so much joy that up-cycling became my new passion

I have just settled in Belgium where I work from home. I hope you like what you see on my page and that it might inspire you to get creative, too!
