Hi, I'm Margreet!

and I love fixing things. I use my skills and creativity to give broken, old or unwanted things another life by repairing and upcycling. The smallest improvement makes not only me happy, but others too. I work from home, which is in Belgium. I have lived in Germany, the USA and the UK but my Dutch roots come in handy, literally. I hope you like what you see on my page and that it might inspire you to get creative.

If you want to leave the handywork to me, by all means have a browse of my website and see what takes your fancy. All items are upcycled by yours truly, breathing new life into loved furniture. If you have an old piece which you would to love to have revitalised, get in touch via the Get a Quote form. We look forward to hearing from you!
