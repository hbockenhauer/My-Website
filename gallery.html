Revitalize Your Furniture with UpCycled Woodcrafts

At UpCycled Woodcrafts, we specialize in transforming your old furniture into stunning, one-of-a-kind pieces. With over 40 years of woodworking expertise, Margreet and her team are passionate about giving new life to your cherished items.

Whether you have a worn-out dresser or a tired armchair, we'll work with you to upcycle it into a beautiful, functional addition to your home!

Get in touch to see how we can transform your beloved belongings from old to new or if browse our shop to see how you can upgrade your home. For inspiration, meander through our works in the gallery.
